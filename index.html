Tree Of Mammalian Embryogenesis

Mammalian embryogenesis is characterized by extraordinarily rapid cellular proliferation and diversification. Although intensively studied, a complete, continuous, systematically ascertained delineation of the cell lineages and trajectories that comprise mammalian development remains elusive. Here we set out to integrate four single cell RNA-seq datasets that collectively span mouse gastrulation and organogenesis. We defined cell types at each of 17 successive stages of development (E3.5 - E13.5) and heuristically connected them with their inferred antecedents and descendants.

  • It contains 45 branchpoints and 337 nodes.
